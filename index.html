Restaurant Ordering System

System Overview

This prototype demonstrates a restaurant ordering system with three main views:

  • Customer View: For customers to browse menu, place orders, and view order status
  • Kitchen View: For kitchen staff to receive orders and update their status
  • Cashier View: For front-of-house staff to manage orders and payments

To test the system:

  1. Start by entering a table number in the Customer View
  2. Place an order as a customer
  3. Switch to Kitchen View to see and update the order
  4. Switch to Cashier View to mark the order as served and process payment
